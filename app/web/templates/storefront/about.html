Our Story

BORN FROM A BELIEF IN CLEANER ROADS

EV Nation was founded with a single conviction — that the future of urban transport in India is electric. Located in Saharanpur, U.P., we saw firsthand the congestion, pollution, and rising fuel costs that everyday commuters face, and decided to do something about it.

We partnered with India's leading electric scooter manufacturers to bring cutting-edge EV technology directly to your doorstep. From compact city riders to powerful long-range models, every scooter in our showroom is handpicked for reliability, performance, and value.

Today, EV Nation proudly serves hundreds of satisfied riders across Saharanpur and the surrounding region — and we're just getting started.
